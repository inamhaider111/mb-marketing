About us

MB Marketing has been working with international pharmaceutical firms ever since its beginning. During all these years this group has succeeded in gaining the trust of farmers and its team by providing them with high quality and international standards products (Pharmaceutical and Neutraceuticals). Through out this period this group has successfully established and spread its infrastructure and distribution network throughout the country. "Never compromise on Quality" policy of this group is the main reason behind trust of farmers and its whole team in it. This group is specialized in import of wide range of Veterinary and poultry products from different countries of the world. Basically this group deals in import and marketing of products. Our supplier Multinational companies have international quality certification.
